About Co-Founders

Co-Founders is the hit Bay Area Musical about an underrated coder from Oakland who hacks her way into the most competitive startup accelerator in Silicon Valley. It sold out its entire 6 week world premiere of 40 performances at the American Conservatory Theater (A.C.T.) in 2025, with over 10,000 tickets, making it one of the highest demanded shows in SF theater history.
